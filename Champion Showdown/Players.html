About Player

ATF's professional career began in the tier-2 scene before he caught the attention of OG in 2022. His breakout performance at multiple Majors showcased his ability to play high-impact offlaners and create space for his team. After joining Team Falcon in 2024, ATF has continued to develop his game, becoming one of the most feared offlaners in competitive Dota 2.

Known for his signature heroes like Mars, Axe, and Primal Beast, ATF excels at creating chaos in team fights and drawing attention away from his cores. His aggressive laning style often puts opponents on the back foot, and his ability to make game-changing plays in crucial moments has earned him respect from players and fans alike.

With Team Falcon, ATF has achieved remarkable success, including multiple tournament victories and top placements at The International. His chemistry with teammates Skiter, Malr1ne, Crit, and Sneyking has created one of the most formidable lineups in modern Dota 2. As one of the youngest players in tier-1 competition, ATF's future in professional Dota looks incredibly promising.
